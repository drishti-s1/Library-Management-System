Departmental History

The Department of Computer Science was formed in the year 1995. The Head Of the Department is Mrs.Rita Sinha as she joined then. The Prominent Teachers of the Department are Rita Sinha, Prity Prakash Deva Sarma, Dr. Fakhar Uddin Ahmed, Manisha Deka, Deepchand Singh, Shilpi Singh and others.
The college follows the curriculum of Gauhati University.There are 3 branches namely under the guidance of the Department. B.Sc, B.Sc-IT, BCA. The College also provides PGDCA under Gauhati University.
The Department's Placement Cell works actively for the welfare of its students.Each year a number of students of the Department get chance into Wipro,TCS, Infosys etc. Many students are even working abroad. The Department sets goals to provide worthy education and works towards making students successful in life.
